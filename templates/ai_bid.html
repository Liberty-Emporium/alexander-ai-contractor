What is this page?

AI Bid Creator is your fastest path from job inquiry to professional quote. Describe the job and get a complete, itemized bid in seconds.

How to Use It

  • 1. Describe the job — Be specific: "Replace 200 sq ft of drywall in a residential living room, water damage, includes priming and painting"
  • 2. Set your location — Labor rates vary significantly by region
  • 3. Add materials if known — or let AI estimate based on job type
  • 4. Review the bid — edit any line items before sending
  • 5. Download or send — get a PDF or send directly to the client

Tips for Winning Bids

  • Add a brief "Why us" section to every bid — it differentiates you from competitors
  • Include a clear timeline and payment terms
  • Price to cover your actual costs + 20-30% margin minimum
Describe the scope in as much detail as you know. The more context you give the AI, the more accurate and professional the bid output will be.
