What is this page?

My Bids is your complete history of every bid you've created. Track status, follow up on open bids, and analyze what's winning.

Bid Statuses

  • Draft — created but not sent to client yet
  • Sent — client has received it, waiting for response
  • Won — job awarded, get to work
  • Lost — client went with someone else
  • Expired — bid window closed with no response

Following Up to Win More Jobs

  • Follow up on Sent bids after 48 hours — a quick "Any questions?" message doubles close rates
  • When a bid is Lost, ask the client why — pricing feedback improves future bids
  • Click any bid to view, edit, or download it as a PDF to send to the client
Review your Won vs Lost ratio monthly. If you're losing more than 50%, your pricing may be off — use the AI Pricing Advisor to recalibrate.
