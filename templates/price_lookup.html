What is this page?

Price Lookup gives you instant regional pricing for labor and materials — searchable by trade, task type, and location. Use it to price any line item accurately.

How to Use It

  • Search by task: "drywall installation", "electrical outlet", "tile floor"
  • Select your state or region — prices adjust to local labor markets
  • Results show: low / mid / high range and unit (per sq ft, per hour, per unit)
  • Use mid-range as your baseline unless your market commands premium rates

Building Accurate Bids

  • Look up every line item in your bid here before setting the final price
  • Add 10-15% to material costs for waste, delivery, and contingency
  • Your labor rate = price lookup rate × your efficiency factor (faster workers = higher margin)
Save your most-used price lookups as bookmarks — flooring, drywall, painting are your bread-and-butter lookups if you're a general contractor.
